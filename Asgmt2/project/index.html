United States Tourist Guide

The U.S. has always attracted many visitors each year.

This visualization project will take you through FOUR main perspectives on 2019 U.S. traveler data.

Who travell to U.S.? When should travell to U.S.?

How does people travel to U.S.? What travelers do during their trip?

U.S. popular Destination

We can see from the map that the east and west of the United States are tourist attractions, with Los Angeles in the west and New York in the east being the most popular. Every year, millions of travelers go there.

Who?

The largest number of people travel to the United States are from Mexico and Canada, and we can guess that both countries are neighbors of the United States. The number of people from these two countries exceeds other countries by a lot.

On the contrary, the least number of Indians travel to the United States.

When?

We can find from the graph that the winter in the United States, which is located in the northern hemisphere, is usually colder in December. Summer is usually from June to August. There is not much difference in temperature in most states.

The coldest of these places is Alaska and the warmest is Hawaii.

How?

During this four-year period, airplanes have been the most preferred mode of transportation for travelers, followed by roads. However, we can also see that very few tourists travel to the United States by water or rail.

What?

Everyone travels for different levels of purpose and they are almost evenly split. But still we can find that most travelers like to Chilling on the beach.
